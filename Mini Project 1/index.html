Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is a symbol of love built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort

The Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Delhi, is an iconic example of Mughal architecture and a symbol of India's rich history.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar, located in Delhi, is the tallest brick minaret in the world and a stunning example of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Hawa Mahal

Located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, the Hawa Mahal or "Palace of Winds" is known for its intricate latticework and unique design.

India Gate

India Gate, located in New Delhi, is a war memorial that honors the soldiers who fought in World War I.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, located in Karnataka, is one of the most magnificent royal palaces in India and an architectural masterpiece.
